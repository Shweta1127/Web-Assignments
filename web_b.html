Animal Info

Animal Name Features
Lion
  • The lion is a well-muscled cat with a long body, large head, and short legs.
  • Size and appearance vary considerably between the sexes.
  • The male's outstanding characteristic is his mane, Manes make males look larger and may serve to intimidate rivals or impress prospective mates.
Wolf
  • The wolf has long and powerful legs, as well as a mighty stamina
  • The wolves usually travel during the night times or in the cool temperatures during dawn and dusk
  • Wolves are very well-equipped for the hunt.
Tiger
  • The tiger is a powerful and colorful species of big cat.
  • Tigers have hair all over their body to insulate, protect and camouflage themselves in their habitat
  • In order for it to survive and thrive in its own habitat, the tiger has powerful razor-sharp teeth, muscular legs.
